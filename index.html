About Sitiri

Our campaign is a CALL TO ACTION, to policy makers, state and non state actors, donors. It is an invitation to stand with women and girls in Kenya , to veil their vulnerabilities from the harshness of poverty, and to cover them with the respect and support they deserve. Together, we can break the silence around period poverty and create a future where every girl can step confidently into her potential, free from embarrassment and full of hope. Join us in making “SITIRI CAMPAIGN” a beacon of change and a testament to our shared humanity. Because every girl deserves to live with dignity, and together, we can make it happen.

Other Programmes

Menstrual Health and Hygiene Awareness Campaigns

We are committed to educating the next generation; we have a school- based program designed to educate school- going kids with information on menstruation and good hygiene practices.

Educational materials

We provide comprehensive educational materials for students. These materials include: Informative Booklets: Easy-to-understand guides on menstrual health and hygiene. These booklets can be used by menstruators and organizations.

47 counties 47 libraries challenge

Sitiri is determined to improve access to reliable information on menstruation and that’s why we are focused on equipping all 47 county libraries in all the counties in Kenya with our menstrual health education booklet.

Libraries visited

1: Nairobi County - Mukuru Kwa Ruben Library .
2: Baringo County - Meisori Library Mariget .
